High Speed CoaXPress 2.0 Camera

HX-A241M-94

Sony CMOS Pregius S IMX530 | 24.5 MP | Global Shutter

1. Product Overview

The HX-A241M-94 is a professional-grade, ultra-high speed industrial camera designed for high-end vision systems. Featuring the **Sony® Pregius S IMX530** global shutter sensor, it delivers **24.5 megapixel** resolution (5328 x 4608) at an impressive **94 fps** through a **CoaXPress 12 (CXP-12) 2-channel** interface. With its back-illuminated pixel structure and massive bandwidth, this model is optimized for high-throughput semiconductor inspection, large-scale electronics assembly, and advanced sports analytics where every frame counts.

2. Key Features

Pregius S Sensor Performance Utilizes the 4th generation IMX530 sensor, providing high sensitivity and excellent image quality with a 2.74 µm pixel size in a 1.2" optical format.
High-Speed CXP-12 Interface The dual-channel CoaXPress 2.0 interface ensures rapid data transmission up to 24 Gbps with minimal jitter and latency.
Global Shutter Accuracy Eliminates motion artifacts, allowing for precise distortion-free imaging of fast-moving objects on automated production lines.
Industrial Ruggedness Encased in a robust housing designed for superior thermal dissipation and high stability in challenging industrial environments.
