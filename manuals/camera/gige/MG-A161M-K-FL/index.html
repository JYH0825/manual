High Performance GigE Vision Camera

MG-A161M/K-FL

Sony CMOS Pregius S IMX542 | 16.1 MP | Global Shutter

1. Product Overview

The MG-A161M/K-FL is a high-resolution industrial camera featuring the advanced Sony® Pregius S IMX542 global shutter sensor. It delivers a high-fidelity 16.1 megapixel (5328 x 3040) resolution at 13 fps via a Gigabit Ethernet interface. With its large 1.1" sensor format and 2.74 µm pixel size, it is optimized for precision-critical inspection tasks in semiconductor manufacturing, high-density PCB assembly, and large-scale metrology applications.

2. Key Features

4th Gen Sony Pregius S Sensor Utilizes the back-illuminated IMX542 sensor to achieve high quantum efficiency and low noise within a 1.1" optical format.
GigE Vision & GenICam Seamlessly integrates with industry-standard software libraries such as HALCON, Cognex, and CVB through full protocol compliance.
Power over Ethernet (PoE) Simplifies system architecture by enabling data transmission and power supply through a single network cable.
Ultra-Compact Design The space-efficient 29 x 29 x 40 mm housing allows for easy installation in restricted industrial environments without compromising performance.
