High Performance GigE Vision Camera

MG-A500M/K-FL

Sony CMOS Pregius S IMX547 | 5.1 MP | Global Shutter

1. Product Overview

The MG-A500M/K-FL is a high-speed industrial camera equipped with the Sony® Pregius S IMX547 global shutter sensor. Providing 5.1 megapixel resolution at a rapid 22 fps through a Gigabit Ethernet interface, it offers the perfect balance of resolution and speed for factory automation, 3D inspection, and medical imaging where high-throughput processing is essential.

2. Key Features

Sony Pregius S Technology Utilizes the 4th generation IMX547 sensor with a back-illuminated structure for superior imaging quality in a compact 1/1.8" format.
GigE Vision Standard Fully compliant with GigE Vision 1.2 and GenICam standards, allowing for seamless integration with universal software libraries.
Advanced PoE Capability Simplifies deployment with Power over Ethernet, reducing cabling complexity while maintaining high data reliability.
Compact Industrial Design A rugged 29x29x40mm housing ensures the camera can be integrated into space-critical machinery without sacrificing thermal performance.
